Pub/Sub smoke: a design with plain cache variables plus a config.xml carrying a
<PubSub> section (publisher with two DataSetWriters AND a self-subscribing
DataSetReader on the same multicast connection). Passing means the generated
Configuration.xsd accepts the PubSub section, the generated Configurator stages
it, and the PubSub engine resolves every address, opens the UDP transport and
starts both roles — the server then survives a uasak_dump client session.
